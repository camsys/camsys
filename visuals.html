Yearly State of Good Repair.

  • Historical and projected data are divided by the black vertical bar.
  • The right y-axis is only used by the black curve, which shows the yearly investment.
  • The left y-axis scales the weighted percentages of assets each year.
  • The green trendline indicates the general trajectory of good assets over the shown historical years.
  • The horizontal water table line is the defined threshold for being in "good repair."

Single-year Investment Breakdown

  • Breaks down the investment required during each year.
  • Click or shift-mouseover on a year on the left.
  • Each layer outward displays the data in a finer grain.
  • Click on any segment to drill down into its sub-groups.
